Add Your Aladtec Schedule to Outlook

San Juan Island Fire & Rescue

Part 1: Get Your Personal Calendar Link from Aladtec

1

Log in to Aladtec

Go to Aladtec and sign in with your credentials.

2

Open Your Schedule

Click on My Schedule in the navigation menu.

3

Click "Share My Schedule"

Look for the Share My Schedule button (usually in the top-right area of the calendar).

4

Create or Select a Calendar Subscription

If you don't have one yet, click Add Subscription and configure what to include:

  • Scheduled Time (recommended)
  • Time Off
  • Trades
  • Events

Click Add to save your subscription.

5

Copy the Calendar Link

Click the Links tab, then click Copy next to one of these options:

  • Calendars using GMT/UTC - Best for Outlook
  • Google Calendar - If you use Google
Important: Your calendar link is private! It contains a unique code that gives access to your schedule. Don't share it publicly.

Part 2: Add to Outlook

Outlook Desktop (Windows/Mac)

  1. Open Outlook
  2. Go to File > Account Settings > Account Settings
  3. Click the Internet Calendars tab
  4. Click New...
  5. Paste your Aladtec calendar link
  6. Click Add
  7. Give it a name like Aladtec Schedule
  8. Click OK

Outlook Web

  1. Go to Outlook Web
  2. Click the Calendar icon
  3. Click Add calendar (left sidebar)
  4. Select Subscribe from web
  5. Paste your Aladtec calendar link
  6. Name it Aladtec Schedule
  7. Choose a color
  8. Click Import
Tip: Your calendar will automatically sync! Outlook checks for updates every few hours. Any schedule changes in Aladtec will appear in your Outlook calendar.
Mobile App: Add the calendar via Outlook Web or Desktop first - it will automatically sync to your Outlook mobile app.

Troubleshooting

Calendar not updating?

Internet calendars sync every few hours. To force a refresh in Outlook Desktop, right-click the calendar and select Refresh.

Can't find the calendar?

In Outlook, look in the left sidebar under Other calendars or Shared calendars. Make sure the checkbox is checked to display it.

Events showing wrong time?

Make sure you copied the Calendars using GMT/UTC link from Aladtec. This ensures times display correctly in your timezone.
