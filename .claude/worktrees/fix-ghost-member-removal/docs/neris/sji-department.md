# DEPARTMENT-SPECIFIC — San Juan Island Fire & Rescue

Facts about the district that inform incident reporting. Not workflow instructions — just local knowledge.

## Apparatus & Crew Mapping

- **Career crew** (Captain, Lieutenant, AO) rides the primary `*31` apparatus together — usually **E31** (engine), sometimes R31 or B31 depending on the call type.
- **Support and standby positions** from the schedule rarely ride first-due rigs — do NOT auto-assign them to E31, M31, or other primary apparatus. Leave them unassigned and ask the user.
- **Tenders** (T33, T36), **ladder** (L31), and other apparatus are typically staffed by volunteers, off-duty personnel, or sometimes standby crew — ask the user.
- **Chief officers** ride BN31 or OPS31.

## Common Incident Patterns

- **Chimney fires** are the most common structure fire (woodstoves, fireplaces). If CAD mentions "chimney", "flue", "woodstove", "fireplace", "creosote", or "chimney fire", use `FIRE||STRUCTURE_FIRE||CHIMNEY_FIRE`. Do NOT default to `CONFINED_COOKING_APPLIANCE_FIRE` unless the CAD specifically mentions a cooking appliance.
- **Vegetation/grass fires** are more common than wildland fires unless CAD indicates a large or spreading wildland fire.
- **Lift assists** are very common — `PUBSERV||CITIZEN_ASSIST||LIFT_ASSIST`.
- **Gas leaks** are usually propane (not natural gas) — the island uses propane tanks, not municipal gas lines.

## Local Geography & Facilities

- **Default city**: Friday Harbor, WA 98250
- **Station**: S31
- **Primary receiving facility**: PeaceHealth Friday Harbor
- **Gas/electric**: OPALCO (electric cooperative) / local propane vendors
- **Mutual aid**: Neighboring island departments (OIFR, etc.) and county resources
